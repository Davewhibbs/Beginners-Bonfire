References

I went above and beyond on this project by researching and taking notes for all of the information in the site from within Dark Souls 3. I didn't use any sources from the internet, this is exactly the lesson I would teach someone if they asked me how to play Dark Souls. I explained every stat and made tables showing how each starting character is optimized. I wanted to take videos from inside the game to add to the combat section, however I ended up not having enough time to gather that along with all of the information. I'm planning to continue working on this project after it's due, just because I found that I got very engrossed in searching through the game for data, and I genuinely want more people to experience what Dark Souls has to offer.
